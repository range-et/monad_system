Typography

IBM Plex Sans (body), IBM Plex Mono (code), IBM Plex Serif (editorial). Carbon type scale.

Display — 54px Light
Hero — 42px Light
Page Heading — 32px SemiBold
Section Heading — 28px SemiBold
Sub Heading — 24px SemiBold
Body text — 16px Regular. The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
Secondary body — 14px Regular. Helper text, captions, and labels.
Overline label — 12px SemiBold uppercase
Monospace — IBM Plex Mono 14px: const color = "#03A9F4";
